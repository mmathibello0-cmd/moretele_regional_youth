Digital Engagement Sessions

Ithute • Tsaya Karolo • Gola

Our Digital Engagement Sessions are designed to help youth learn more about our church while connecting with one another through interactive online activities.

About Our Sessions

Sessions are hosted regularly and are open to every youth member in the Moretele Region. They combine learning, discussion and interactive activities to make participation enjoyable and meaningful.

Topics We Explore

Church History

Discover the history and heritage of our church.

Maboko

Learn and understand Maboko and their significance.

Mme Morongwa le Badimo

Grow your understanding through guided learning and discussion.

Church Hymns

Learn church hymns and the meaning behind them.

Church Monuments

Explore important places and monuments within our church.

Interactive Discussions

Participate in discussions, polls, Kahoot and Q&A sessions.
