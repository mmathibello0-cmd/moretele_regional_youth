Digital Engagement Sessions

Ithute • Tsaya Karolo • Gola

Welcome to the official Digital Engagement Programme of the Moretele Region Youth. Our sessions are designed to help every young person grow in knowledge, faith and participation through structured online lessons, discussions, quizzes and interactive activities. Re ithuta Baebele, hisitori ya Bokonyana, Maboko, difela, le tsotlhe tse di agang tumelo ya rona. Re dumela gore fa re ithuta mmogo, re gola mmogo mo tumelong le mo tirelong ya Modimo.

How Our Engagement Sessions Work

Every Digital Engagement Session follows a structured learning programme. Members first complete the online lesson, then participate in live discussions, Kahoot quizzes, question-and-answer sessions and fellowship with other youth members. Maikaelelo a programme eno ke go dira gore mongwe le mongwe a nne le sebaka sa go ithuta, go botsa dipotso, go tsaya karolo le go gola mo kitsong ya Bokonyana le Tshenolo The Holy Church.

Typical Engagement Programme

Every Engagement Session follows the same structure to ensure that everyone has an opportunity to learn, participate and grow together.

Opening Prayer

5 Minutes

We begin by opening the session in prayer and committing our learning to Modimo.

Welcome

5 Minutes

Welcome members, introduce the topic and explain the objectives of the session.

Main Lesson

20 Minutes

Study the selected lesson together while learning important teachings and church history.

Kahoot & Activities

15 Minutes

Test your understanding through Kahoot quizzes, polls and other interactive activities.

Discussion

15 Minutes

Ask questions, share ideas and learn from one another through guided discussion.

Closing

10 Minutes

Review the lesson, share key takeaways and close with prayer.

Our Vision

The Digital Engagement Programme exists to preserve the teachings, history and heritage of Bokonyana while making learning accessible to every young person. Through every lesson, discussion and activity, we hope to strengthen faith, encourage participation and build a united generation that proudly carries the values of Tshenolo The Holy Church.
