About Moretele Region Youth

Learn • Participate • Grow Together

Le amogetse mo Moretele Region Youth. This platform was created to connect young people from all 18 diSetsha within the Moretele Region through learning, fellowship, participation and digital engagement.

Ka Rona | About Us

Moretele Region Youth ke setšhaba sa basha ba ba tswang mo diSetsheng tsotlhe tsa Kgaolo ya Moretele. Maikaelelo a rona ke go kopanya basha, go rotloetsa participation, unity le learning ka go dirisa technology go thusa basha go gola mo kitsong ya kereke le mo tumelong. Re dumela gore fa re ithuta mmogo, re gola mmogo.

Meet the Moretele Region Youth Committee

The Moretele Region Youth Committee is made up of dedicated individuals who have accepted the responsibility of serving the youth across all diSetsha within the Moretele Region. Re direla legodimo ka boikanyego, re aga bonngwe, re rotloetsa go tsaya karolo, mme re thusa basha go gola mo Bokonyaneng ka boeteledipele, lorato le tirelo.

Our Mission | Maikaelelo a Rona

Go aga community ya basha ba ba nang le kitso, ba ba tsayang karolo mo ditirong tsa kereke, mme ba tswelele go gola mo tumelong, mo tirelong le mo boeteledipeleng.

Our Vision | Ponelopele ya Rona

To build one connected regional youth community where every young person feels welcomed, valued and encouraged to learn, serve and grow. One Region • 18 diSetsha • One Youth Community.

What We Learn | Re Ithuta Eng?

Maboko

Mme Morongwa le Badimo

Discovering important church monuments and places of significance.
