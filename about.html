About Moretele Region Youth

Learn • Participate • Grow Together

Re amogetse mo Moretele Region Youth. This platform was created to connect young people from all 18 diSetsha within the Moretele Region through learning, fellowship, participation and digital engagement.

Ka Rona | About Us

Moretele Region Youth ke setšhaba sa basha ba ba tswang mo diSetsheng tsotlhe tsa Kgaolo ya Moretele. Maikaelelo a rona ke go kopanya basha, go rotloetsa participation, unity le learning ka go dirisa technology go thusa basha go gola mo kitsong ya kereke le mo tumelong. Re dumela gore fa re ithuta mmogo, re gola mmogo.

Our Mission | Maikaelelo a Rona

Go aga community ya basha ba ba nang le kitso, ba ba tsayang karolo mo ditirong tsa kereke, mme ba tswelele go gola mo tumelong, mo tirelong le mo boeteledipeleng.

Our Vision | Ponelopele ya Rona

To build one connected regional youth community where every young person feels welcomed, valued and encouraged to learn, serve and grow. One Region • 18 diSetsha • One Youth Community.

What We Learn | Re Ithuta Eng?

Church History

Learning about the history and heritage of our church.

Maboko

Understanding Maboko and their importance in our church.

Mme Morongwa le Badimo

Learning about the teachings and significance of Mme Morongwa le Badimo.

Church Hymns

Learning church hymns, their meanings and their history.

Church Monuments

Discovering important church monuments and places of significance.

Interactive Learning

Kahoot, discussions, live polls, question-and-answer sessions and other engaging activities.

Our Values | Melaometheo ya Rona

💛 Faith

Tumelo ya rona e re etelela pele mo go tsotlhe tse re di dirang.

🤝 Unity

Re kopane re le basha ba Moretele Region.

📚 Learning

Go tswelela go ithuta ka kereke ya rona le tsotlhe tse di e agang.

🌱 Growth

Go tlotla mongwe le mongwe le go aga tikologo ya lorato.
