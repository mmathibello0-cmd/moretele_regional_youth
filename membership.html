Why Become a Member?

Community

Become part of a growing community of youth from all 18 diSetsha in the Moretele Region.

Learn

Take part in learning sessions covering Church History, Maboko, Mme Morongwa le Badimo, Church Hymns and more.

Activities

Receive updates about youth meetings, events, conferences and Digital Engagement Sessions.

Fellowship

Meet new friends, build relationships and grow together in faith.
