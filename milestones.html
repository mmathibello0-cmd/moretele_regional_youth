Milestones

Growing Together, One Step at a Time

Every milestone represents our commitment to learning, participation and unity. We celebrate progress as one youth community.

What Are Milestones?

Instead of competing against one another, Moretele Region Youth celebrates shared achievements. Every member and every Setsha contributes towards our regional growth. As we reach each milestone together, we celebrate the dedication and participation of our youth community.
