Who We Are

The Moretele Region Youth Committee exists to strengthen unity, encourage active participation and create opportunities for young people across all Setshas to learn, grow and serve together. Through digital engagement sessions, educational activities, and collaborative initiatives, we aim to build a connected, knowledgeable and vibrant youth community.
