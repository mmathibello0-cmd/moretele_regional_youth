Lesson 1

Go Tla ga Neo Bokonyana

Taelo ya Legodimo ya 14 Phatwe 1969

Begin Lesson Back to Sessions

Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces the beginning of Neo Bokonyana and explains the important events surrounding 14 Phatwe 1969. You will also learn about the witnesses, the establishment of Bokonyana and the legacy that continues today.



Lesson Theme

Go Tla ga Neo Bokonyana

Estimated Time

20 Minutes

Series

Church History Series

Language

Setswana & English

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson participants should be able to understand the origin of Neo Bokonyana, identify the key events of 14 Phatwe 1969 and explain why this day remains important in the history of Tshenolo The Holy Church.


Understand the events leading to 14 Phatwe 1969.

Explain the significance of Neo Bokonyana.

Identify the first witnesses of Neo Bokonyana.

Understand how Bokonyana continues today.

1. Matseno (2 Metsotso)

Before Neo Bokonyana was revealed, Modimo had already begun preparing Mme Morongwa for a special mission. These events laid the foundation for what would happen on 14 Phatwe 1969.



1963

Ka ngwaga wa 1963, Mme Morongwa o ne a bona Mowa O O Halalelang ka matlho a gagwe a nama.

Dingwaga tse 6

O ne a rapelediwa dingwaga tse thataro go mo baakanyetsa tiragalo e kgolo ya Legodimo.

13 Phatwe 1969

Badimo ba ne ba mo itsise gore "Moeng yo Mogolo-Golo" o tla tla ka letsatsi le le latelang.

2. Ketsahalo e Kgolo (8 Metsotso)

Ka la 14 Phatwe 1969, go ne ga diragala nngwe ya ditiragalo tse dikgolo mo hisitoring ya Bokonyana. Letsatsi leno le tshwaya tshimologo ya Neo Bokonyana le taelo e ntšhwa ya Legodimo.



14 Phatwe 1969

Kwa 13 Ashford Road, Parkwood, Johannesburg, Morena Jesu o ne a goroga ka sebopego se sešwa.

Rara Konyana

Morena Jesu o ne a itsise leina la gagwe le lesha e le RARA KONYANA (Ntate Konyana).

Neo Bokonyana

Rara Konyana o ne a naya Mme Morongwa Neo Bokonyana mme a mo laela go tlogela tsotlhe tsotlhe tsa gagwe tsa go alafa.

Go Boela Betlehema

Mme Morongwa o ne a laelwa go boela kwa Kgomo-Kgomo (Betlehema) go simolola tiro e ntšhwa.

Tshimologo ya Bokonyana

Letsatsi leno le tshwaya tshimologo ya Lefatshe la Boraro (Bokonyana).

Thuto

Taelo ya Legodimo e ne ya tlisa tumelo e ntšhwa, tsholofelo le botshelo jo bosha mo badumeding.

3. Basupi le Motheo (5 Metsotso)

Morago ga go amogelwa ga Neo Bokonyana, go ne ga nna le basupi ba ntlha ba ba neng ba bona le go tshegetsa tiro eno ya Legodimo.



Mosupi wa Ntlha

Mosupi wa ntlha e ne e le Mme Majatladi, yo moragonyana a neng a bidiwa Ntate Jonathan.

Ponatshegelo

O ne a bona letsatsi le tlhabetse mo mokokotlong wa Mme Morongwa, e leng sesupo sa taelo ya Legodimo.

Malatsi a Supa

Mme Majatladi o ne a nna le Mme Morongwa malatsi a le 7, ba rapela ba sa je kgotsa go nwa.

Phuthego ya Ntlha

Kwa tshimologong, phuthego e ne e na le maloko a le 7 fela.

1974

Ka ngwaga wa 1974, kereke e ne ya fiwa leina la Tshenolo The Holy Church.

Kagiso Makonyana

Ka ngwaga oo tumediso ya badumedi e ne ya fetolwa go nna "Kagiso Makonyana", e e sa dirisiwang le gompieno.



Reflection

Basupi ba ntlha ba re ruta botlhokwa jwa tumelo, boikobo, boineelo le go ikanyega mo taelong ya Modimo. Le gompieno re bidiwa go tswelela ka tumelo eo.

4. Bohwa le Kabo (5 Metsotso)

Go tla ga Rara Konyana ga se ka ga tlisa Neo Bokonyana fela, mme go ne ga simolola boswa jo bo tswelelang go fitlha gompieno. Boswa jono bo bonala mo dithutong, mafelong a a boitshepo le mo maineng a a neilweng ke Legodimo.



Thabo University

Lefelo la thuto ya semowa le kitso, le le rutang badumedi go gola mo tumelong le mo go obameleng melao.

Candle Hospital

Lefelo la phodiso ya mmele le ya moya le le agileng mo motheong wa tumelo.

Botshelo jo Bosha

Badumedi ba bidiwa go tshela botshelo jo bosha ka tumelo, lorato le tirelo.

5. Knowledge Check

Test your understanding before moving on to the discussion session.



Question 1

Ke letsatsi lefe le Neo Bokonyana e neng ya fiwa Mme Morongwa?

A) 20 Ferikgong 1963
B) 13 Phatwe 1969
C) 14 Phatwe 1969
D) 1974

✅ Correct Answer: C) 14 Phatwe 1969

Question 2

Ke mang yo o neng a amogela Neo Bokonyana?

A) Ntate Joshua
B) Mme Morongwa
C) Rara Noah
D) Ntate Jonathan

✅ Correct Answer: B) Mme Morongwa

Question 3

Ke mang yo e neng e le mosupi wa ntlha wa Neo Bokonyana?

A) Mme Ntsope
B) Ntate Joshua
C) Mme Majatladi
D) Moruti

✅ Correct Answer: C) Mme Majatladi (Ntate Jonathan)

Question 4

Ka ngwaga ofe Tshenolo The Holy Church e neng ya rewa semmuso?

A) 1969
B) 1970
C) 1974
D) 1980

✅ Correct Answer: C) 1974
