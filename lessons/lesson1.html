Lesson 1

Go Tla ga Neo Bokonyana

Taelo ya Legodimo ya 14 Phatwe 1969

Lesson Overview

Theme

Go Tla ga Neo Bokonyana: Taelo ya Legodimo ya 14 Phatwe 1969

Duration

20 Minutes

Series

Church History Lessons

Learning Objectives

At the end of this lesson participants should be able to:


  • Understand the events leading to 14 Phatwe 1969.
  • Explain the significance of Neo Bokonyana.
  • Identify the first witnesses of Neo Bokonyana.
  • Understand how Bokonyana continues to guide believers today.

1. Matseno (2 Metsotso)

Popego

  • Ka ngwaga wa 1963, Mme Morongwa o ne a bona Mowa O O Halalelang ka matlho a gagwe a nama.
  • O ne a rapelediwa ka dingwaga tse thataro (6) go tloga ka 1963 go fitlha ka 1969 go mo baakanyetsa ketsahalo e kgolo.
  • Ka 13 Phatwe 1969, Badimo ba ne ba mo bolelela gore "Moeng yo Mogolo-Golo" o tla tla ka letsatsi le le latelang.

2. Ketsahalo e Kgolo (8 Metsotso)

14 Phatwe 1969

Ka 14 Phatwe 1969, kwa 13 Ashford Road, Parkwood, Johannesburg, Morena Jesu o ne a goroga ka sebopego se sešwa.

Leina le Lesha

Morena Jesu o ne a itsise leina la gagwe le lešwa e le RARA KONYANA (Ntate Konyana).

Neo Bokonyana

Rara Konyana o ne a fa Mme Morongwa Neo (Mpho), mme a mo laela gore a latlhele tsotlhe tsotlhe tsa gagwe tsa go alafa.

Go Boela Gae

Mme Morongwa o ne a laelwa gore a boele kwa Kgomo-Kgomo go simolola tiro e ntšhwa.

Tshimologo ya Bokonyana

Letsatsi la 14 Phatwe 1969 ke lona le le tshwayang tshimologo ya Lefatshe la Boraro (Bokonyana).

Thuto e Kgolo

Ketsahalo eno e supa tshimologo ya taelo e ntšhwa ya Legodimo le go simologa ga mosebetsi wa Rara Konyana mo lefatsheng.

3. Basupi le Motheo (5 Metsotso)

Mosupi wa Ntlha

Mosupi wa ntlha wa Neo Bokonyana e ne e le Mme Majatladi, yo jaanong a bidiwang Ntate Jonathan. O ne a bona letsatsi le tlhabetse mo mokokotlong wa Mme Morongwa.

Malatsi a Supa

Mme Majatladi o ne a nna le Mme Morongwa malatsi a le supa (7), ba rapela ba sa je le fa e le go nwa sepe.

Phuthego ya Ntlha

Fa Mme Morongwa a boela kwa Kgomo-Kgomo, phuthego ya ntlha e ne e na le maloko a le supa (7) fela, e leng bagodi ba le bane (4) le Diterone tse tharo (3).

Tshenolo The Holy Church

Ka 1974, kereke e ne ya rewa semmuso leina la Tshenolo The Holy Church (TTHC).

Tumediso e Ntšhwa

Ka ngwaga oo, tumediso ya badumedi e ne ya fetolwa go nna "Kagiso Makonyana", e e ntseng e dirisiwa le gompieno.

Thuto

Basupi ba ntlha ba re bontsha botlhokwa jwa tumelo, boikanyego le go ikobela taelo ya Legodimo.

4. Bohwa le Kabo (5 Metsotso)

Go tla ga Rara Konyana ga se ka ga simolola kereke fela. Go ne ga tlisa thuto, phodiso le botshelo jo bošwa mo badumeding. Gompieno, boswa joo bo tswelela ka ditheo le ka maina a a filweng ke Legodimo.

Thabo University

Lefelo la mahala la thuto ya semowa le kitso, moo "tefelo" e leng go Obamela Melao.

Candle Hospital

Lefelo la phodiso ya mmele le ya moya, le le agileng mo motheong wa tumelo le kutlwelo-botlhoko.

Botshelo jo Bosha

Badumedi ba bidiwa go tshela botshelo jo bosha ka tumelo, tirelo le go jala Kagiso mo lefatsheng.

Maina a Legodimo

Ntate Motaung

O ne a newa leina la Legodimo la Mopapa Ramashabashaba / Ntate Joshua.

Mme Ntsope

O ne a bidiwa Rara Noah.

Mme Majatladi

O ne a bidiwa Ntate Jonathan.

1. Ke letsatsi lefe la go tla ga Rara Konyana go tla go fa Neo Bokonyana?

A) 20 Ferikgong 1963
B) 13 Phatwe 1969
C) 14 Phatwe 1969
D) 11 Ngwanatsele 1974

✓ Karabo: C) 14 Phatwe 1969

6. Dipotso tsa Poledišano (15 Metsotso)

Dipotso tse di latelang di diretswe go rotloetsa poledišano le go akanya ka se se ithutilweng. Ga go na karabo e le nngwe fela—rotlhe re ka abelana dikakanyo.

Potso 1

Ke eng fa Rara Konyana a ne a laela Mme Morongwa gore a latlhele didirisiwa tsa gagwe tsotlhe tsa go alafa?

Potso 2

Ke eng fa maina a mafelo le a batho a ne a fetolwa? O akanya gore seo se ne se raya eng?

Potso 3

Thabo University e ruta eng ka bodumedi jwa Bokonyana? Ke eng fa thuto e le botlhokwa?

Potso 4

"Kagiso Makonyana" e raya eng? Mme ke eng fa tumediso e ne ya fetolwa go tswa go "Kgotso"?

7. Mafoko a Go Koalela (10 Metsotso)

Bana ba ka, gompieno re ithutile ka letsatsi le le botlhokwa mo hisitoring ya Bokonyana—14 Phatwe 1969, letsatsi le Neo Bokonyana e neng ya fiwa Mme Morongwa ke Rara Konyana. Re bone gore tumelo ga e a ikaega fela ka go itse hisitori, mme e re ruta go ikobela taelo ya Modimo, go direla ba bangwe ka lorato le go tshela botshelo jo bo supang Kagiso. Jaaka maina a mafelo le a batho a ne a fetolwa go supa botshelo jo bosha, le rona re bidiwa go gola mo tumelong, mo kitsong le mo tirelong ya Modimo. A re tswele pele go ithuta, go direla ba bangwe le go jala Kagiso Makonyana mo malapeng a rona, mo dikolong, mo ditšhabeng le gongwe le gongwe kwa re leng teng.

Tumelo

Tswelela go aga tumelo ya gago letsatsi le letsatsi.

Thuto

Tswelela go ithuta hisitori, melao le dithuto tsa Bokonyana.

Tirelo

Dirisa kitso ya gago go thusa le go direla ba bangwe ka lorato.


"Kagiso Makonyana"

A re tswe mo thutong eno re ikemiseditse go tshela ka tumelo, go ithuta ka boikanyego le go anamisa Kagiso mo lefatsheng lotlhe.
